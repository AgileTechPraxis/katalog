## Prime Factors

Factorize a positive integer number into its prime factors.


> Example:
> 
>| number | prime factors |
>| ------ | ------------- |
>| 2      | [2]           |
>| 3      | [3]           |
>| 4      | [2,2]         |
>| 6      | [2,3]         |
>| 9      | [3,3]         |
>| 12     | [2,2,3]       |
>| 15     | [3,5]         |
>
